EDGE Teacher Guide: Interpreting Model Output

This short guide explains how to act on the EDGE model's predicted probability of misconception (p).

  • p ≥ 0.70: Very likely misconception. Show targeted remediation now (worked examples, step-by-step).
  • 0.40 ≤ p < 0.70: Possible risk. Schedule practice within 24 hours (spaced repetition).
  • p < 0.40: Likely ready for challenge. Offer a harder problem or move forward.

Adjust thresholds based on teacher preference: lower thresholds increase recall (catch more struggling students), higher thresholds increase precision (fewer false alarms).
